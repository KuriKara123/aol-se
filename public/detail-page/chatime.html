Product Details

Chatime is a global bubble tea (also known as boba tea) franchise that originated in Taiwan in 2005. It quickly expanded internationally and became one of the largest bubble tea chains in the world. Here's a breakdown of Chatime: Bubble Tea: Bubble tea is a popular Taiwanese tea-based drink that typically contains tea, milk, sugar, and chewy tapioca pearls (also known as boba). It comes in a variety of flavors and can be customized with different toppings, such as fruit jelly or pudding. Chatime: Chatime specializes in serving a wide range of bubble tea flavors, offering customers the ability to customize their drinks according to their preferences. They have an extensive menu that includes milk teas, fruit teas, smoothies, and more. Menu: Chatime's menu features a variety of options, including classic milk teas like the traditional pearl milk tea, as well as fruit-infused teas such as mango green tea or passionfruit black tea. They also offer toppings like tapioca pearls, pudding, grass jelly, and aloe vera to add texture and flavor to the drinks. International Presence: Chatime has expanded rapidly since its inception and has a significant presence in many countries around the world, including the United States, Canada, Australia, the United Kingdom, Malaysia, and many others.
